## 摘要
训练了一个神经网络来学习大气对流中水汽、液态水和冰相之间的质量交换。该网络基于LFRic模型区域配置的对流解析输出进行训练，该配置使用了多矩微物理参数化方案(CASIM)。学习这些相交换的损失函数是在热平衡和机械平衡(所有相具有相同温度和压力)以及机械不平衡(所有相具有不同的吉布斯自由能)假设下制定的。网络输出确定水汽、液态水和冰的交换，以保持质量守恒，并根据网络输出确定熵的变化，以保持能量守恒。该神经网络以热力学一致的方式实现于一个二维垂直切片不连续伽辽金模型中，该模型模拟湿非静力大气，以模拟亚公里分辨率对流的三相云形成。结果与基于物理的三相湿过程在热力学平衡下的表示结果进行了比较。

## Abstract
A neural network is trained to learn the mass exchanges between vapour, liquid and ice phases in atmospheric convection. The network is trained on convection resolving output from a regional configuration of the LFRic model with a multi-moment microphysics parameterisation (CASIM). The loss function for learning these phase exchanges is formulated under the assumptions of thermal and mechanical equilibrium (same temperature and pressure for all phases), and mechanical dis-equilibrium (different Gibbs free energies for all phases). The network outputs determine the exchanges of vapour, liquid and ice so as to conserve mass, and the resulting change in entropy is determined from the network outputs so as to conserve energy. The neural network is implemented in a thermodynamically consistent manner within a 2D vertical slice discontinuous Galerkin model of a moist, non-hydrostatic atmosphere in order to simulate the formation of three-phase clouds for convection at sub-km resolution. The results are compared to those from a physics based representation of three-phase moist processes at thermodynamic equilibrium.
