## 摘要
我们证明了在维度$d=2,3$下，针对Oberbeck-Boussinesq系统的连续数据同化方法的收敛性。我们的工作假设是参考解的有界性，而同步解在弱意义下满足方程。主要工具是随机问题的相对能量不等式。

## Abstract
We show convergence of a continuous data assimilation method for the Oberbeck-Boussinesq system in the dimension $d=2,3$. Our working hypothesis is boundedness of the reference solution, while the synchronized solution satisfies the equations in a weak sense. The main tool is the relative energy inequality for stochastic problems.
