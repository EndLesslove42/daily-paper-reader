## 摘要
大涡模拟（LES）湍流模型通常无法捕捉化学放热效应以及由此产生的预混火焰中湍流的调制，这凸显了需要一个能够在广泛物理范围内保持准确的框架。我们开发了一种增强的涡粘性封闭模型，基于深度神经网络，使用伴随优化和可微编程与LES解联合校准，确保与偏微分方程（PDE）的一致性。研究了多个目标函数和训练方法，并评估了每个模型在广泛达姆科勒数范围内进行插值和外推的能力。相对于Smagorinsky模型基线，最佳神经网络模型将LES原始变量的后验误差改善了25-50%，解析的雷诺应力和标量通量改善了60%以上。关键的是，该模型能够泛化到不同达姆科勒数范围，即使在样本外条件下也能保持稳定性和准确性。这些结果表明，与PDE一致的深度学习封闭模型可以恢复湍流预混火焰LES中的平均场和解析湍流统计量，因此可以为湍流燃烧建模提供一个广泛适用的框架。

## Abstract
Large-eddy simulation (LES) turbulence models often fail to capture the effects of chemical heat release and the resulting modulation of turbulence in premixed flames, underscoring the need for a framework that remains accurate across a broad range of physical regimes. We develop an augmented eddy-viscosity closure, based on deep neural networks calibrated jointly with the LES solution using adjoint-based optimization and differentiable programming, ensuring consistency with the governing partial differential equations (PDEs). Several objective functions and training methods are examined, and each model is assessed for its capability to interpolate and extrapolate across a wide range of Damköhler numbers. Relative to the Smagorinsky-model baseline, the best neural network model improves a posteriori errors in the LES primitive variables by 25-50% and in the resolved Reynolds stress and scalar flux by more than 60%. Crucially, the model generalizes across Damköhler number regimes, maintaining stability and accuracy even for out-of-sample conditions. These results demonstrate that PDE-consistent deep learning closures can recover both mean fields and resolved turbulence statistics in LES of turbulent premixed flames and can therefore provide a broadly applicable framework for turbulent combustion modeling.
